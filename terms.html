Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2024

1. Terms of Service

1.1 Acceptance of Terms

By accessing or using the Grants.gov website, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service and all applicable federal laws and regulations. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use this website.

1.2 Eligibility

To use this service, you must be:

  • A U.S. citizen or legal permanent resident
  • At least 18 years of age
  • Legally able to enter into contracts
  • Not barred from receiving federal funds

1.3 User Responsibilities

As a user of Grants.gov, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Maintain the security of your account credentials
  • Notify us immediately of any unauthorized access
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Not attempt to defraud or mislead the government
⚠️ Important: Providing false information on a federal application is a violation of 18 U.S.C. § 1001 and may result in fines, imprisonment, or both.

1.4 Application Processing

Grants.gov processes applications on behalf of federal agencies. Submission of an application does not guarantee approval or funding. All applications are subject to review and approval by the relevant federal agency.

1.5 Fees

The processing fee covers administrative costs including application review, verification, and customer support. This fee is non-refundable once the application review process has begun.

2. Privacy Policy

2.1 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, date of birth
  • Financial Information: Bank account details for fund disbursement
  • Application Data: Grant applications and supporting documents
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

2.2 How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Process and evaluate grant applications
  • Disburse approved funds
  • Communicate about application status
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Comply with federal reporting requirements

2.3 Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • PCI compliant payment processing
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

2.4 Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with:

  • Federal agencies processing your application
  • Law enforcement as required by law
  • Payment processors for fee transactions
  • Banking partners for fund disbursement

2.5 Data Retention

Application records are retained in accordance with federal records schedules. Most records are kept for a minimum of 7 years as required by law.

3. Additional Legal Information

3.1 FOIA Requests

Information submitted through Grants.gov may be subject to disclosure under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). Certain information may be exempt from disclosure.

3.2 Section 508 Compliance

Grants.gov is committed to making its electronic information accessible to people with disabilities in accordance with Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act.

3.3 No FEAR Act

Grants.gov complies with the Notification and Federal Employee Antidiscrimination and Retaliation (No FEAR) Act.

3.4 Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified terms.
